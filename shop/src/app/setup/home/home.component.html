Decentralized. Forever. Your content stays.

The internal data of your shop is placed on the Ceramic Network and will be resolved via its URL. Thats all what is needed to display this shop Dapp to your customers. The content you sell lives on IPFS or Arweave.

Your customer can be assured that the digital content they buy will be there, and can be re-downloaded all the time.
