Guidelines and rules when using this shop software.

This shop should be used to allow people to distribute their content freely and safe whill optimizing their revenue by removing intermediate parties. There are a few usage guidlines for this shop protocol. Please stick to them to make this journey a nice experience for everyone involved.

  1. Only sell content you own the rights
    Violating copyrights is a serious crime and does not help the creator community. Please only sell digital content that you are legally allowed to do. You must either be the creator or own the permission to do so. If in doubt, please check with a legal expert first.
  2. No hateful content
    Please refrain from selling items that are hurtful to certain groups of people, contains racism, sexism or any other form of derogatory content.
  3. No erotic of pornographic content
    As of now there is no safe way on the blockchain to verify the age of the creator or the seller. To protect minors it is therefore necessairy to abstain from selling any form of pornographic or erotic material.

This list is very likely not exhaustive and with time it additional items or rules might get added. Please check from time to time and re-evaluate the items you sell to stick to those rules.
