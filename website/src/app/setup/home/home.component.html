This Webshop is build with the digital Content Creator in mind. No fees or issues with payment providers anymore. The Web3 shop "lives" truely in the cloud and its data is owned by you, and you alone. Proceeds go 100% to your wallet. Your content, your earnings.

Decentralized. Forever. Your content stays.

The internal data of your shop is placed on the Ceramic Network and will be resolved via its URL. Thats all what is needed to display this shop Dapp to your customers. The content you sell lives on IPFS or Arweave.

Your customer can be assured that the digital content they buy will be there, and can be re-downloaded all the time.

Eeen, Eeeef, Tees?! To the next level.

Every sold digital good is represented via a NFT that governs the access to the digital files via the Lit Protocol. Yes, purchased items can be re-sold, just like physical goods!

Even the ownership of the your shop is engraved into a special Ownership-NFT. Someone else should take over ownership of your shop? Just send or sell him your Ownership-NFT.

We Layer 2! Cheap and simple.

In order to keep the costs low the software utilizes Layer 2 technology to execute all the Smart Contract logic inside a rollup. This reduces energy consumption substantially and makes it environment friendly. Usability and simplicity is a number one design priority of this software.

FAQ

Will the code be open source?

Yes! As soon as the codebase has matured and we are on the Beta, the code will be open sourced on Github under the GPL v3.

Will it support other rollup techs like zkSync?

Definitely. The choice fell to Arbitrum because it was pretty established and had a good support from other tools that are required for the shop to work (Lit Protocol and Bundlr Network). As soon as those support zkSync too this rollup can be supported as well.

Will there be a token?

Nothing planned yet. Discuss on Orbis Discussion Group the potential use cases of a token.

Is there Vice Capital involved?

Nope, this is (or better will be, as the Github repository is not yet published) just a regular Open Source project. Completly developed by enthusiasts. So in case there will ever be a token at some point in time, it will not go to vice capital investors but fully to the community.
